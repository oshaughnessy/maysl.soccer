Summer's here, the World Cup is on, and it's time to get ready for another soccer season!

**Registration for fall 2026 is now open**, and we'd love to see you back on the field.

[**Sign up now &raquo;**]({% link _pages/registration.md %})

## Coaches &mdash; Jump In Early!

Our teams only happen because of dedicated volunteer coaches like you. If you're planning to coach this fall,
**please register now** so we can get rosters organized and teams formed in time. Early signups make a huge
difference!

While you're at it, get a head start on your [coaching requirements]({% link _pages/coachreg.md %}) &mdash; things
like background checks, SafeSport training, and any certification courses. Getting these done early means no
last-minute scrambles before the season kicks off.

## Travel Players &mdash; Don't Wait!

Travel soccer gets going in **July**, so if your player is looking forward to the travel experience,
**now is the time to register**. Spots fill up quickly and rosters need to be set before teams are set.

## Everyone's Welcome

Whether it's your first season or your tenth, we're so glad you're here. MAYSL is a community league and
every player, coach, and family makes it special. Let's make fall 2026 the best one yet!

Questions? Reach out on our [contact page](/contact/).
