[Cal North is changing
league ages](https://www.calnorth.org/news/important-update-cal-north-shifts-to-seasonal-year-age-groups-in-2026-2027).

In years past, we would use birth year to determine league age, which affects the division your players go into.
With Cal North's change for the 2026-27 season, league age more closely aligns with school years. The age of
a player before Aug 1 of the starting year will be their league age.

Here's an easy way to figure it out. Ask yourself,

> How old is my player by August of the season's starting year?

For example, this year's season starts in 2026. For a player born March 1, 2015:
1. _How old will she be on Aug 1, 2026?_ **11.**<br />
    (She turned 11 on March 1, she'll still be 11 in Aug.)
2. Therefore, _she's league age 11_, and fits into an **Under 12** division.
